A console application that helps rangers that work in a park see information about tasks they are to perform
and apply for such tasks.

1. Ranger can log in.

2. Ranger can see their personal information, such as:
	name, email, ...
	rank
	number of tasks assigned, ...

3. Ranger can see all unfinished tasks assigned to them - highest priority first.
4. Ranger can see all unfinished tasks assigned to them - most recent first.

5. Ranger can see all their finished tasks.

6. Ranger can see all unassigned tasks - highest priority first.
